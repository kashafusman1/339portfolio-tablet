What is Halal @ Umich?

Halal @ Umich is a guide to halal food resturants in the city of Ann Arbor, lets eat halal together!

What is Halal Food?

Halal, in Arabic means: lawful or permitted. In reference to food, it is the dietary standard in accordance with the religion of Islam.
